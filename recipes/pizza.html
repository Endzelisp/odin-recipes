Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

How to prepare an incredible pepperoni pizza at home

Do you ever wonder how I make my pepperoni pizza at home? Then you will love this step-by-step recipe. A home-made pizza will nourish your belly and your soul. You will know from scratch how to accomplish it.

Ingredients

  • The Sauce
    • 1.5 Kg Fresh ripe plum tomato
    • 2 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
    • ½ large yellow onion, minced
    • 1 medium clove garlic, minced
    • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper optional
    • ½ teaspoon dried oregano leaves
    • Salt to taste
  • The Dough
    • 200 gr Wheat flour
    • 120 gr Water
    • ½ teaspoon Yeast
    • ½ teaspoon Salt
    • 1 teaspoon White Sugar
    • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • Topping
    • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
    • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
    • Some leaves fresh basil, optional
    • fresh oregano, optional

Steps

The Sauce

  1. Cut the tomatoes into wedges and reserve them
  2. In a saucepan add the oil and put medium heat
  3. Add the onion and garlic and cook them until the onion become almost translucent. mix constantly with a spatula.
  4. Add the black pepper if you decided to use it
  5. Add the tomato and adjust the stove to low heat
  6. Add dried oregano
  7. Cook at low heat until the sauce is thick

The Dough

  1. Put all the wheat flour in a bowl.
  2. Separate the flour and creates a hole in the center.
  3. Add the water.
  4. Add all the sugar and the yeast into the water and dissolve it completely.
  5. Start mixing while gradually incorporating flour from the borders.
  6. knead the dough for 3 or 4 minutes just enough to be sure all ingredients are mixed but don't care about how it looks
  7. At this point the dough has a rough appearance, let it rest for half an hour.
  8. Now the dough is more tender, add the salt and oil and knead for approximately 5 minutes until everything is well mixed
  9. Let the dough rest for half an hour. knead it again a couple of minutes and let it rest again for another half an hour
  10. At this point the dough is ready.

The Pizza

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 450 and 500 degrees F or as high as your oven allow it
  2. Flatten the dough with your hands or with the help of a rolling pin and put it in a pizza oven pan
  3. Add enough sauce to cover the surface but don't exceed the amount, also don't reach the borders
  4. Let the dough leaven some 10 minutes
  5. Add the toppings staring with cheese, put some hidden pepperoni slices and add more cheese
  6. put the pizza in the oven.
  7. A pizza may take between 8-15 minutes to bake
  8. Add fresh basil leaves and oregano
